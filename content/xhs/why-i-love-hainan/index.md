# Why I think Hainan is the paradise of china:

When we took a vacation in Hainan, I thought I accidentally discovered the
paradise of China. I remember telling my girlfriend, "We must come here every
year!" I don't remember the last time I felt this free.

We spent a few days in Haikou and then moved to stay in a 5 star hotel in Sanya.
It was so nice and we got so many vouchers for massages, restaurants, and
coconut chicken soup places.

I really understand why Chinese people call it the Hawaii of China, it really is
a paradise. Our hotel was next to the main beach in Sanya and every night you
could walk on the beach while people were throwing fireworks and doing light
shows. You really feel relaxed and like you can wind down.

But why do I really love Hainan?? It's the birthplace of my favorite Chinese
drink: Coconut Palm (椰树牌椰汁). I love this drink so much that we even went to
the factory! (I thought it was a museum, so we drove there and tried to enter,
but they didn't let us in... Look at the last picture ahahhah)

China has a lot of rules and I feel like most nature attractions are very
organized - you need to buy a ticket and follow the route. But in Sanya we just
rented a scooter, bought some snorkeling masks, and went around exploring the
beaches. We went to one beach and snorkeled a bit far from the shore, and then
we spotted a big rock with so many fish around it. It was amazing, it was one of
the few times I actually felt like I was experiencing true nature in China.

Maybe I just liked Hainan because it made me feel free!

Every day we just went to a different beach, put our hammock between the trees,
got some takeaway food and drinks, and just enjoyed life.

What do you think about Hainan? Are there any other places in China that make
you feel this way?

We went in the winter. Hainan people - how do you survive the summer here?
