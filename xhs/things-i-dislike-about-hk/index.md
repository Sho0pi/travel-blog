# why I dislike about Hong Kong life

I used to live in Hong Kong for about a year. In the end I decided to leave (you
can read the full reasons in my other post).

I have to be honest, I loved Hong Kong, but disliked it at the same time.

Living in Hong Kong, you feel like you are fighting for space with everyone.
Walking in the narrow streets can be challenging sometimes, especially during
rush hour.

Eating in a restaurant is weird as well, especially coming from a very warm
country. I used to small talk with every shopkeeper and waitress, but now it
feels like everything is rushed. People want me to just finish eating as fast as
possible and leave.

The weirdest thing for me in Hong Kong was people saying "I hate my job". I
don’t think I’m ignorant, but this saying is so weird to me. Why do you want to
spend so much time in a job you hate? How can you wake up every day to a job you
don’t like that gives you so much stress? Back home, a job is just a job.
Sometimes there are better days, sometimes more stressful days, but none of the
people I know ever told me they hate their job. Never.

If someone in my country hates their job, they just quit. I feel like we value
our time much more than the people who work in Hong Kong - this was really hard
for me to accept, and still is.

I’m really asking: Why do you want to spend 9-5 or even 9-9 every day in a place
you hate?

I feel like people really need to embrace life, talk to the strangers around us,
because we are all here on the same planet, on the same earth. No one really
knows what he is doing, so we might as well just enjoy the ride! Be nice to
strangers, and don’t let any boss tell you how to live your life.

For me it was too hard to accept this lifestyle. I traveled the world for 1 year
before coming here, so it was such a shock. I knew I wouldn’t last long in this
city, but I didn’t expect to leave this early.

When I say I also loved Hong Kong, I really mean it. I want to write a similar
post in the future talking about the things I loved, so you will not only see my
one-sided perspective.

Now I’m living in Singapore. It also has its challenges, but I feel like the
pace is way slower. You can walk outside, look at the otters swimming in the
river, and not just rush to work or home.

What do you think about this lifestyle? How can you handle it so well? Maybe I’m
just not Hong Kong material.
